Tacos

Description

Simple weeknight tacos with seasoned beef and fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Brown beef and stir in seasoning.
  2. Warm tortillas.
  3. Fill and top with veggies, cheese, and salsa.
