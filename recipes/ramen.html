Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup with endless flavor variations. This version is quick, rich, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Soft-boiled egg
  • Sliced pork
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Warm broth in a pot.
  2. Cook noodles per package directions.
  3. Assemble noodles, broth, and toppings in a bowl.
