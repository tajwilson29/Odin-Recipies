Chocolate Cake

Description

A classic chocolate cake that’s rich, soft, and perfect for celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Whisk dry ingredients; add wet and mix smooth.
  3. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
